Lab report delivered in a hyperdoc
For my anatomy class, a hyperdoc was used to give students instruction about the sheep heart dissection. A dissection manual and picture was linked so that students could see the names of the parts of the brain. Students were also asked to take three pictures of the brain, view from top, view from bottom and internal structure, and to label parts of the brain. This activity seemed to help students work together to make a lab report and increase student participation.
